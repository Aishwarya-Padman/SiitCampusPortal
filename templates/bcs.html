B.Sc(Ecs)



This is a three-year bachelor degree course in computer science aimed at developing computer professionals versatile in the use of computers, mostly in the business world. The emphasis is to develop professionals as programmers, system analysts, database administrators, documentation officers, etc.


Eligibility for Admission of B.Sc(Ecs):


A candidate must have passed H.S.C (10+2) in only science stream awarded by University/State Board in any technical or Vocational Stream.
